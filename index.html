Who We Are

A band of people
finding God
and each other.

HumiliTeam Network isn't your typical church group. We're a youth network based in Olongapo City built on three things: loving God deeply, loving people fiercely, and making disciples who actually make disciples.

But we're also just people looking for real friends. If you're searching for community — whether you're deeply rooted in faith or just curious — you belong here. Walk in any time. No performance required.

What We Stand On

Three pillars.
One mission.

01 ✦

Love God

Faith isn't a performance — it's a relationship. We dive into scripture, ask hard questions, and pursue a God who's bigger than any answer we have.

02 ◈

Love People

Belonging before believing. Everyone is welcome at any stage of their journey. We don't gatekeep grace — we just show up for each other.

03 ◐

Make Disciples

The goal was never to grow a group. The goal is to grow people who go and do the same thing — love well, lead humbly, multiply.

You're Already Welcome

Not a service.
A family.

You don't need to have it together to show up. HumiliTeam is for people who are figuring it out — people who want Godly friends, not just acquaintances. People who want depth, honesty, and a place they can actually belong.

For the Seeker

Curious about God but not sure where to start? Come ask your questions. We don't have every answer — but we'll look for them together.

For the Lonely

Looking for real friends — not just followers? We're a group of people who genuinely show up for each other. Come as you are.

For the Faithful

Rooted in your faith and ready to go deeper? We study together, pray together, and challenge each other to keep growing.

For the Lost

Going through something heavy? You don't have to carry it alone. This is a safe place — judgment isn't something we do here.
